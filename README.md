# Geo Mentorship Initiative

**Empowering underserved, unheard, and underrepresented youth** with guidance, clarity, and confidence through mentorship that is practical, compassionate, and free.

> “Every young person deserves someone who says: *You belong here.*”

---

## 🚀 Mission

To provide **mentorship, direction, and access** to students and youth who lack support systems—especially **first‑generation, international, rural, and minority** communities—so they can build **strong futures, meaningful careers, and confident identities**.

---

## 💡 What We Offer

- **1:1 Mentorship** — goal-setting, academic/career guidance, accountability  
- **Group Circles** — monthly mentorship sessions & Q&A  
- **Open Office Hours** — weekly drop-in support  
- **Workshops & Toolkits** — study abroad, scholarships, resumes/portfolios  
- **STEM & Geospatial Tracks** — GIS, UAVs/drones, Remote Sensing, research skills  
- **Human Support** — listening, confidence building, community care

Everything here is **free** and **community-first**.

---

## 👤 Who This Is For

- First‑generation students  
- National and International students  
- Youth from underserved/underrepresented communities  
- Students entering **GIS, UAVs, Surveying, Fluvial Geomorphology, Geomatics, Geoinformaics, Remote Sensing, STEM**  
- Anyone who feels **lost, unheard, or without guidance**

If that’s you: **You’re in the right place.**

## 🗓 Program Structure

- **Orientation:** Understand your story and goals  
- **Action Plan (3 months):** 1–3 achievable milestones  
- **Check-ins:** Monthly (or open office hours)  
- **Community:** Group sessions, peer support  
- **Progress Review:** Reflect, refine, celebrate wins

This structure keeps mentorship **focused and sustainable**.

## 🌈 Code of Conduct

We are committed to a **safe, inclusive, and respectful** environment.  
See **CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md** for expectations and reporting.

Key principles:
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination  
- Respect lived experiences; practice active listening  
- Protect privacy and dignity of mentees

---

## 🔐 Privacy & Safety

- No sharing of mentee personal stories without explicit consent  
- Mask identities in public docs (use initials or anonymized data)  
- Use private channels for sensitive conversations  
- Follow university/school guidelines where applicable
